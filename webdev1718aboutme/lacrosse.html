Lacrosse

Lacrosse, while not the most popular sport, is a great sport nonetheless. It is nicknamed the "fastest game on two feet." Lacrosse has been compared to hockey a lot of times, as many hockey players at Lane also play Lacrosse. Many great schools have great lacrosse programs which could mean that lacrosse can be the little extra that could get you into college.

    Lacrosse Accolades
  • The national sport of Canada
  • The fastest game on two feet
  • Run about 52x farther than the average baseball player
  • Played to prevent wars back during Native American times
  • Requires the ability to work with a team to be competitive, increasing social skills and making friendships that could benefit you later.
  • Need to have high endurance to be able to play effectively, increasing fitness
  • Need to be aggressive and willing to take a hit to play, creating tougher people
  • Should be an olympic sport
  • You can get scholarships for college
